Energy Optimisation as a Service

Make lower energy spend a monthly discipline, not a one-off project.

The problem with one-off projects

Savings drift. Settings get changed, equipment ages, habits slip — and the gains quietly disappear. Without ongoing accountability, energy performance becomes a project that gets shelved.

How EOaaS works

A rolling monthly cycle — review performance, recommend next-best actions, report clearly, verify savings. Someone owns the outcome, not just the initial report.

  • Monthly performance review across your estate
  • Prioritised next-best-action recommendations
  • Stakeholder reporting in plain English
  • Savings verification against agreed baselines

What's included each month

We track what changed, what worked, and what needs attention next. Monitoring data (where available) feeds into a continuous improvement loop rather than a one-off audit.

Who it suits

Operators who want energy performance owned and maintained across multiple sites — especially after a Discovery Assessment Report and Roadmap have identified the priority actions. This can include estates that need ongoing evidence after ESOS-assessor reviewed findings.

When it may not

Single-site operators with limited plant complexity may not need monthly oversight. We will recommend EOaaS only where ongoing optimisation makes commercial sense.
