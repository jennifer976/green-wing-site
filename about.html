Who we work with

Hospitality, leisure, manufacturing and commercial property operators across the UK — especially multi-site estates where the same issues repeat branch after branch.

Our story

Green Wing has worked on commercial energy optimisation since 2009. We started with a simple observation: most energy waste is operational long before it is technological — hidden in plant settings, control schedules and daily routines that nobody has time to question.

Today we help operators of pubs, restaurants, hotels, gyms, factories and commercial property portfolios find that waste, rank the fixes, and prove the savings with evidence their finance teams can trust.

We have assessed more than 25 technologies across real operating sites. Not every solution suits every estate — and we will tell you when something will not pay back.

What we believe

Four principles that guide every engagement.

Measure first

We observe and profile before we recommend. Bills tell you how much you spent — not where it went or why.

Stay independent

No product line dictates our roadmap. Technology appears only when site evidence supports it.

Prove savings

Every roadmap item includes a measurement route — baseline, verification method and site context agreed upfront.

Fix operations first

Quick operational wins often outperform capital projects. We rank actions by payback and complexity.
