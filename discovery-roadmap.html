Service 02

Discovery Assessment Report and Roadmap

Your findings, written clearly and turned into a plan the board can act on.

What it contains

A plain-English Discovery Assessment report, followed by every opportunity ranked by savings potential, payback period, implementation complexity and measurement method.

  • Clear assessment findings from the site visit
  • Ranked list of operational and technology actions
  • Payback and savings estimates with site-context qualifiers
  • Complexity and dependency flags for each line
  • Measurement route defined upfront for every recommendation

Why ranking matters

It tells you what to do first — the quick operational wins before the capital projects. Boards and site teams need decisions, not another document that sits on a shelf.

The measurement promise

No item makes the Discovery Assessment Report and Roadmap without a way to prove the result. That is how savings stay credible after the work is done — baseline agreed, verification method defined, site context documented.

ESOS-assessor review where required

If your organisation needs ESOS compliance evidence, the relevant assessment findings and report can be reviewed by a qualified ESOS assessor. The result is a document that supports compliance conversations while still giving site teams a practical action plan.

When it suits

Estates that have completed a Discovery Assessment (or equivalent site evidence) and need a board-ready prioritisation. Often the bridge between finding waste and implementing fixes or EOaaS.

When it may not

If you need a generic compliance report without intent to implement, the Discovery Assessment Report and Roadmap will feel like overkill. We build it for operators ready to act.
