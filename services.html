How we work

Services

A clear path from finding waste to proving it's gone — delivered as three connected services you can start at any point.

Onsite Discovery Assessment

What it is A structured site visit where we inspect the major energy loads — refrigeration, HVAC, heating, lighting, power quality — and the way they're actually operated day to day.

What you get A clear picture of where energy is being wasted, separated into operational fixes (no/low cost) and technology opportunities (with suitability flagged).

Why it's different We measure and observe before we recommend. No technology is proposed without a reason it suits your site.

Discovery Assessment Report and Roadmap

What it is The Discovery findings written up as a clear Discovery Assessment Report and Roadmap, with every opportunity ranked for action.

What you get A practical assessment report plus each opportunity scored by savings potential, payback, complexity and how the result will be measured.

Why it's different Every line has a measurement route — so savings can be proven, not just promised.

Energy Optimisation as a Service (EOaaS)

What it is Ongoing optimisation as a monthly service rather than a one-off project.

What you get Monthly review, next-best-action recommendations, reporting and savings verification across your estate.

Why it's different Energy performance is treated as a continuous discipline, with someone accountable for keeping it on track.

How the three services connect

From a site visit to decisions your team can act on — and savings you can prove. Where ESOS evidence is required, the report can be reviewed by a qualified ESOS assessor.

Step 01

Discover

We walk the site, inspect the major loads, and identify where money is leaking through equipment, settings and routines.

Step 02

Prioritise

You receive a Discovery Assessment Report and Roadmap: what we found, what to fix first, what it can save, what it depends on, and how to prove the outcome.

Step 03

Optimise

EOaaS keeps the work alive with monthly review, next-best actions, reporting and savings verification across your estate.

Typical engagement timeline

  • Week 1 — Site visit: Discovery Assessment on your first site (or representative site from a multi-site estate).
  • Week 2–3 — Findings summary: Plain-English report of operational fixes and technology opportunities with suitability flags.
  • Week 4–6 — Discovery Assessment Report and Roadmap delivery: Assessment report plus ranked action plan with payback, complexity scores and measurement routes for each line.
  • Ongoing — Verification: Implementation support and savings verification; EOaaS for estates wanting continuous optimisation.
