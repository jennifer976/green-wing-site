An energy audit answers: "What did we find?" An opportunity roadmap answers: "What do we do first, what will it save, and how will we prove it?"

Boards and site teams need the second. Ranked actions, payback logic, complexity flags and a measurement route for every line — that's what makes energy work stick.

A typical audit produces a comprehensive document that may sit on a shelf. Our [Energy Opportunity Roadmap](../discovery-roadmap.html) is built for action: every item ranked, every saving qualified, every result verifiable.
