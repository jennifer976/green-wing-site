Rising energy costs, increasing sustainability expectations, and tighter operating margins mean energy is no longer just another business expense. For hotels, gyms, pubs, restaurants, and commercial landlords, managing energy has become a strategic priority.

At **Green Wing Energy Solutions**, we’ve spent the last few months listening to our clients and refining how we support them. Today, we are proud to launch our new website and a streamlined service model designed to take the guesswork out of sustainability.

We aren't installers. We don't have a bias toward one specific technology. Instead, we act as your single point of contact, navigating the market for you. We typically identify opportunities for significant annual savings, often reaching well into the thousands and scaling significantly higher depending on the property size and energy usage.

Here is how the new Green Wing works for you.

---

## 1. The [Discovery Assessment](https://greenwingenergysolutions.com/services): Clarity in Hours, Not Weeks

Most businesses know they could be more efficient, but they don’t know where the "leaks" are. Is it the HVAC system? The cellar cooling? The lighting?

Our **Discovery Assessment** is the first step in our partnership. We spend 2 to 4 hours on-site at your facility. We aren't there to sell you a product; we are there to observe, measure, and understand your specific energy profile through a practical site assessment.

Whether you manage a boutique hotel or a high-traffic gym, our experts look at the physical realities of your operation. We identify the low-hanging fruit and the long-term opportunities that generic energy brokers often miss. By the time we leave your site, we have the data needed to build your custom strategy from a focused commercial energy review.

---

## 2. The [Discovery Roadmap](https://greenwingenergysolutions.com/solutions): Your 48-Hour Strategy

Data is useless if it’s buried in a 50-page technical manual that sits in a drawer. You need a plan you can actually act on.

Within 48 hours of our site visit, we aim to deliver your **Discovery Roadmap**, subject to holidays or sickness. This is a branded, concise report that translates our findings into clear business outcomes.

![](https://cdn.marblism.com/KOW09u6kLQd.webp)

**What’s inside the Roadmap?**
*   **Prioritised Actions:** We rank energy-saving measures by ROI and impact.
*   **Financial Projections:** Clear estimates of your annual savings. We typically identify opportunities for significant annual savings, often reaching well into the thousands and scaling significantly higher depending on the property size and energy usage.
*   **Vetted Quotes:** Because we have a network of trusted suppliers, we manage the quote process for you. You don't have to chase five different installers; we bring the best options to your desk.
*   **Project Management:** Once you approve a path, we mediate between you and the installers, ensuring the job is done right and on time.

You can learn more about how we structure these reports on our [Solutions page](https://greenwingenergysolutions.com/solutions).

## 3. [EOaaS: Energy Optimisation as a Service](https://greenwingenergysolutions.com/services)

The biggest barrier to energy efficiency is often the upfront cost. Even if a project pays for itself in three years, finding the capital today can be difficult for a busy restaurant or commercial landlord.

This is why we’ve introduced **EOaaS (Energy Optimisation as a Service)**.

EOaaS is a monthly subscription model that transforms energy efficiency from a capital-intensive project (CapEx) into a predictable operating expense (OpEx).

![](https://cdn.marblism.com/VqJkwQZl-v5.webp)

With EOaaS, you get:
*   **Immediate Savings:** Because the service fee is often lower than the energy savings generated, many businesses see a positive cash flow from month one.
*   **Ongoing Monitoring:** We don't just "fit and forget." We act as your outsourced energy optimisation partner, constantly monitoring performance and adjusting your strategy as your business grows.
*   **Risk Transfer:** We continue monitoring performance and work with our delivery partners to resolve issues quickly, helping ensure your investment delivers the expected results.

For businesses that want to stay "future-ready" without depleting their cash reserves, EOaaS is the most logical path forward. Check out our [Services page](https://greenwingenergysolutions.com/services) for a deeper breakdown of the EOaaS model.
