Finance teams have heard savings promises before. Three things separate credible claims from marketing:

1. **Baseline** — agreed period, normalised for weather or trading where relevant
2. **Measurement route** — meter, sub-meter or calculated verification method defined upfront
3. **Site context** — what changed, what didn't, and why the figure is attributable

Every item in a Green Wing [roadmap](../discovery-roadmap.html) includes all three before it becomes a recommendation. That is how savings stay credible after the work is done — not just at sign-off.
