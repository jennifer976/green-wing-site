Solutions

The technologies we assess and deploy when a site's conditions suit them. We stay independent — a solution only appears in your report and roadmap if the measurement says it belongs there.

Each technology is assessed for suitability first. We will tell you honestly when something will not pay back on your site. Explore our services or arrange an energy review to find out what fits yours.

Cooling & refrigeration

Refrigeration & Cooling Optimisation

Reduces compressor and cooling energy on suitable commercial refrigeration and HVAC systems through operational tuning and targeted interventions.

Typical sites: restaurants, hotels, food manufacturing, retail with significant cold-chain load. Assessed during an Onsite Discovery Assessment.

Technik 2

Bottle-fridge optimisation for hospitality and retail — reduced cooling energy while preserving product quality and display temperatures.

Best suited to bar, restaurant and convenience formats with multiple bottle fridges running extended hours.

Power & electricity

Voltage Optimisation

Cuts wasted electricity where incoming voltage is higher than equipment needs — common on UK commercial sites with elevated supply voltage.

We verify incoming voltage and load characteristics before recommending. Typical savings of 8–15% where conditions suit.

FilterPro — Power Quality & Waste Energy Filtration

Whole-site filtration that improves power quality and reduces waste energy on mixed-load sites with diverse electrical equipment.

Suits commercial property, manufacturing and multi-use sites. Subject to load profile and baseline measurement.

Panoramic Power Monitoring

Circuit-level monitoring that makes energy use visible and verifiable across your estate — essential for proving savings and spotting drift.

Often paired with Energy Optimisation as a Service for ongoing verification.

Heating & combustion

EndoTherm

Heating-system additive that improves heat transfer and reduces gas or heating energy on closed-circuit wet systems.

We test suitability on your heating circuits first. Common on pub, hotel and care-home estates with wet heating.

Dynamic Burner Management

Optimises burner operation to reduce fuel use on combustion-based heating systems without compromising comfort or process requirements.

Assessed against your plant profile and operating schedule during discovery.

Magnetic Fuel Conditioners

Improves combustion efficiency on gas, LPG and oil heating systems where site conditions and equipment age suit the intervention.

We assess boiler condition and fuel type before inclusion in your report and roadmap.

Lighting & visibility

LED Lighting

Lower-energy lighting with better control where existing lighting is inefficient, over-lit or running outside trading hours.

Often combined with operational scheduling fixes identified during discovery — technology alone is rarely the whole answer.
