Onsite Discovery Assessment

The fastest way to see what your bills can't show you.

The problem with bills

Energy bills tell you how much you spent, not where it went or why. A Discovery Assessment closes that gap.

What happens on site

We walk the building, inspect the major loads, and observe how equipment is actually run — not how it was designed to run. We look at refrigeration, HVAC, heating circuits, lighting schedules, power quality and back-of-house plant.

What you walk away with

A plain-English summary of where waste is occurring, split into operational fixes and technology opportunities, each with an indication of savings and how they'd be verified.

  • Site walkthrough and plant inspection report
  • Operational quick wins identified and prioritised
  • Technology opportunities with suitability flags
  • Indicative savings ranges with measurement routes

When it suits

Operators of hospitality, leisure, manufacturing and commercial-property sites — especially multi-site estates where the same issues repeat. Ideal as the starting point before a full Energy Opportunity Roadmap.

When it may not

If you need a compliance-only ESOS report with no intent to act on findings, a Discovery Assessment is not the right fit. We focus on actionable outcomes, not shelf-ware reports.
